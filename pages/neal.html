About Neal's Yard

Neal's Yard is a small alley in London's Covent Garden between Shorts Gardens and Monmouth Street which opens into a courtyard, named after the 17th century developer, Thomas Neale.

With plenty of coffee shops, restauarnts and stores that promote ethical values (including Neal's Yard Remedies, Neal's Yard Dairy, Casanova & daughters and Wild Food Cafe), Neal's Yard has just about everything you need!
