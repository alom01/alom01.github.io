About Word On The Water

Since 2011, Regents canal has been home to a floating bookbarge.

The 1920s Dutch barge has been beautifully transformed into a floating bookshop. It used to travel along Regent's Canal, but has now taken up permanent residence by Granary Square in King’s Cross. As well as keenly priced books they boast a woodburning stove, friendly dog and occasional live jazz performances.
